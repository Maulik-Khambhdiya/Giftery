Refund Policy

At Giftery, we want you to be completely satisfied with your gift purchases. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward refund and return process, subject to the following terms: Please read these Terms of Service carefully before using our website. If you do not agree to these terms, you may not access or use the Service.

  1. Eligibility for Returns
    • Unused and Unopened Items: We accept returns for products that are unused, unopened, and in their original condition within 30 days of the delivery date.
    • Damaged or Defective Items: If your item arrives damaged or defective, we will offer a full refund or replacement at no additional cost. Please notify us within 7 days of receipt.
    • Gift Cards: Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be returned.
  2. How to Initiate a Return

    To begin a return, please contact our customer support team at support@giftery.com. Provide your order number and the reason for the return. You may be asked to provide photos of the item (in case of damage or defect).

  3. Return Shipping Costs
    • For items that are returned due to our error (e.g., damaged, defective, or incorrect items), Giftery will cover the return shipping costs.
    • For other returns (e.g., change of mind or incorrect order), the customer is responsible for the return shipping costs.
  4. Refund Process

    Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

  5. Exchanges

    We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. If you'd like a different item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

  6. Non-returnable Items

    Some items may not be eligible for returns, including:

    • Custom or personalized items
    • Perishable products (e.g., flowers, food)
  7. Order Cancellations

    Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase. After 24 hours, the order may have already been processed and shipped, making cancellation impossible.

  8. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or need further assistance, please reach out to us at support@giftery.com or call our customer service team at (555) 123-4567.
