Who We Are

Established amidst the chaos of the Pandemic, The Gift Studio is a reservoir of cherishing every moment with your family, friends and corporate connections and reaffirming the importance of relationships. According to husband-wife and business partners Shashwat and Shivika Goenka, of the RP Sanjiv Goenka Group, the gifting sector lacked organised and credible players that offer well-curated gifts as per customers’ requirements. Bridging this gap by bringing expert curation and customer personalisation together is what gave birth to the idea of The Gift Studio. Our curation includes flowers, wedding gift hampers, gourmet food, gadgets, art and fashion accessories. We also have a line of hampers curated by renowned personalities including artists Bose Krishnamachari and Paresh Maity as well as fashion designer Anamika Khanna. Headquartered in Mumbai, we deliver all over India, with same-day delivery services available in the metro cities of Mumbai, Kolkata, Pune, Bangalore, Delhi and Gurgaon. In less than 3 years, The Gift Studio has demonstrated to be a one-of-its-kind gifting company, continuously garnering the trust and loyalty of its customers. This rise is by virtue of our exclusively created hampers and broad-ranging gift options.

Our Purpose

With people giving and receiving generic gifts or reused presents, the gift-giving gesture has become somewhat monotonous. We, at The Gift Studio, aim to redefine this gifting landscape with a wide range of personalised, premium and delightful gift options, limited edition specials and beyond. Our goal is to make the gifting experience unforgettable for both the person giving the gift and the person receiving it. That is why we bring you hampers, flowers, cakes and many other gift options that are carefully and lovingly crafted for every occasion, every relationship and to match every kind of unique choice and personality. The cherry on top is our Build Your Own Hamper feature, where you can wrap the warmth of your love in a customised, assorted gift basket.

Our Corporate Clients

The significance and requirement of corporate gifting has increased in the past few years. It is an opportune time to encourage and appreciate employees, working staff, associates and business partners with unique gifts. A heartfelt gift will be a big boost for the morale and togetherness of a team. We have a huge list of corporate clients who trust us with every kind of office, colleague and employee-related gifting needs. Our clientele includes the likes of Accenture, Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC, Amazon, Apollo, Bank of Baroda, BBC Global News India, Spotify India, Samsung India, Sony India, Tata Capital Financial Services, and The Indian Express, to name a few.
